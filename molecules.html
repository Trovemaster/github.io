Molecules of ExoMol

ExoMol

Methane hot opacity:
Line list 10to10

is the new CH4 line list [PNAS, 111, 9379 (2014)], which contains just under 10 billion transitions. The 10to10 line list has already been demonstrated to revolutionise the accuracy of T-dwarf models and characterisa-tion of atmospheres of exoplanets and cool stars.

HOOH:
A hot line list for hydrogen peroxide AYTY

contains 10.3 million rotational-vibrational states and around 10 billion transition frequencies and is applicable to temperatures up to T = 1500 K.

Ahmed F. Al-Refaie et al. Mon. Not. R. Astron. Soc., 448, 1704 (2015)

Phosphine:
Line list SAlTY

contains 16.8 billion transitions and is suitable for simulating spectra up to temperatures of 1500 K [Clara Sousa-Silva et al. Mon. Not. R. Astron. Soc., 446, 2337 (2015)]
